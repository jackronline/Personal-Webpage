About

I'm an aspiring UX/UI & Web Designer based in Barcelona, Spain.

My whole life, I have focused on seeing the beauty in everything. I apply that same outlook to my work by designing and building intuitive and beautiful online spaces. Some of my other self-proclaimed titles include amateur coder, casual photographer, fitness junkie, and car enthusiast.

Bio

Specializing In

With professional training in marketing, and an extensive background in B2C sales, I have had the unique opportunity to refine my skills in both the behind the scenes creative branding, and face-to-face with the buyer.

Since then, I have left the automotive sales field after 10 years to pursue my passion with a creative career. Now based out of Barcelona, Spain, I am focused in the field of UX, UI, and Web Design. I plan to incorporate my love for arts and photography into my daily work. I want to bring my own signature and beauty into the world in the form of appealing and beautiful digital spaces.
